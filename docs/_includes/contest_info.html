{{ site.data.contest.year }} Contest Information

This year is going to be unusual; our contest will be fully online on March 6, 2021 at 12 NOON MST. Each team member will use their own pc although the details are to be determined.

Coaches: Please participate in the pre-qualifier at: https://icpc.global/regionals/finder/North-America-Qualifier-2020 participation in this event will increase the number of teams we get to promote to the online North America Championship.

There are NO FEES this year. You must register early so you can get a t-shirt in time for the contest.

Rules for eligibility will be as if the student were applying in November 2020. See here.

Dates for the North America Championship and the World Finals will be later in 2021 than usual. Note that the 2020 World Finals is planned for June 2021 in Moscow, one year later than originally scheduled. So, the 2021 World Finals will be late in the year, likely October or November.
